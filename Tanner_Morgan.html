Tanner Morgan

Minnesota, Junior, #2

Height: 6'2" | Weight: 216 lbs | 40-yard Dash: 5.06 | SPARQ Score: 69.0

Positives:

Morgan has a high football IQ, and is a great leader on and off the field. He has the talent and the potential to be a solid starter in the NFL, with his good accuracy and above average arm. He can make plays with his feet as well, and the athletic ability will help him to be the more modern NFL QB. He knows how to convert on third down and his awareness inside and outside the pocket will help him greatly.

Negatives:

Morgan does have a bit of trouble reading a defense, and isn't the best decision maker, which leads to some forced throws and some interceptions. He needs to start setting his feet more on throws outside the pocket, instead of just simply trying to throw on the run.

Summary:

Morgan, especially for being just a junior at a school that isn't known as much for producing top-tier QBs, is a young prospect with a lot of raw potential. He could become a great NFL quarterback with some work in some specific areas, and maybe learning for a year or two behind an experienced signal caller would benefit him in the long run. There is no lack of confidence or character in Tanner Morgan, which should make him fun to watch at the next level. I have no doubt in my mind that with the right system, he could become the next great dual-threat QB.

Final Grade:

7.36
